Instant Noodles

Difficulty : ★★☆☆☆

Cook Time : 15 minutes

Ingredients :
• 1 x packet of instant Noodles
• Anything else you like to add to your instant noodles

Directions :
Boil 500ml water in your preferred boiling water holder. Once it has boiled, throw in the seasoning packet and whatever additional ingredient you have chosen and wait for them to cook. Once they are cooked, throw in your instant noodles for around 3-4 minutes depending on how strong your noodles are.
